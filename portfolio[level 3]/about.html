About Me

I’m Rabi Bhagat, a B.Tech Computer Science student at Maharishi Markandeshwar University, set to graduate in 2027. Originally from Nepal and currently based in India, I’m passionate about solving complex problems through code. My expertise lies in web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and mobile app development using Flutter.

With a strong foundation in programming and a drive for continuous learning, I specialize in creating responsive, user-friendly digital solutions. My coursework includes web and mobile app development, and I’m proficient with tools like VS Code, Android Studio, and GitHub.

Although I’m early in my career, I’ve worked on projects like a signature-making app, an e-commerce frontend, and a number guessing game in C. I’m excited to collaborate on innovative projects and bring creative visions to life!
